The Koding Story

Koding is a San Francisco based technology company that is eliminating wasteful tasks from the world of software development and creating a simple way to be more collaborative and productive.

A Better Developer Experience

18 Million VMs Spun Up

Developers can instantly spin up development environments and collaborate in real time with teammates on their codebase.

1,000,000+ Users

Over one million developers use Koding to simply their development process and work seamelssly in the cloud.

Millions of hours saved

Koding’s instant dev environments allow teams to save precious hours on setup and spend more time coding.
